Who We Are

The standard Pakistan's talent market has been missing.

Pakistan has over 4 million freelancers. International clients have no credible way to verify their skills. Employers get burned. Talented professionals get overlooked. The cycle keeps repeating.

PFSVC was built to break that cycle. We run structured skills assessments, document verification, and human interviews — then issue an official badge that stands up to scrutiny anywhere in the world.
